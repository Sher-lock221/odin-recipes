Brown Rice Breakfast Porridge

Description

Brown rice porridge, also known as congee when referring to the savory Asian version, is a hearty and comforting dish made by boiling brown rice in a large amount of water until it becomes soft and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup 2% low-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons dried blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Â¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine cooked brown rice, milk, blueberries, honey, and cinnamon in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low; simmer for 20 minutes.

  2. Whisk hot rice mixture, 1 tablespoon at a time, into egg to temper until about 6 tablespoons incorporated.

  3. Beat egg in a small bowl.

  4. Stir tempered egg, butter, and vanilla into rice mixture.

  5. Continue cooking over low heat to thicken, 1 to 2 minutes.
